Privacy Policy

Quality Of Life: Health · Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Quality Of Life: Health app (the “App”) collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Data you create stays on your device

Your supplement stack, health goals, reminder times, and daily intake logs are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this information to our servers. The App has no user accounts.

2. Optional Google Drive backup

If you choose to use Backup & Restore, your stack, goals, and logs are saved to a private “app data” folder in your own Google Drive, accessible only to this App. We never see or store this data. You can delete it at any time from your Google account.

3. Analytics and diagnostics

To understand how the App is used and to improve it, we use third-party analytics (Mixpanel and Google Firebase Analytics). These may collect:

  • App usage events (screens viewed, features used, supplements added/removed)
  • Device and app information (model, OS version, app version, language)
  • A device advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID) and app-instance identifiers

This data is used for analytics and product improvement only. We do not sell your personal data, and the App does not show third-party ads.

4. Push notifications

We use Google Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver reminders and occasional service messages. Supplement reminders you set are scheduled on your device. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

5. Permissions

  • Notifications — to show supplement reminders.
  • Schedule exact alarms — to fire reminders at the time you choose.
  • Internet — to load the supplement catalog and analytics/diagnostics.

6. Third-party services

The App relies on services provided by Google (Firebase, Google Play Services, Google Drive) and Mixpanel. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Children

The App is intended for adults and is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your choices

  • Use “Clear all data” in the App to erase your stack, goals, logs, and custom supplements.
  • Disable notifications or revoke permissions in your device settings.
  • Reset your advertising identifier in your device’s privacy settings.
  • Delete the App’s Drive backup from your Google account.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
